What will you learn?

This course addresses the cultural and team management aspects of product and service management; provides an overview of the tools and technologies which support service management; and demonstrates how to integrate management practices into end-to-end value streams.

Culture and Collaboration

Learn how to foster a service-oriented culture within your organization. Promote collaboration between teams and departments to deliver services.

Information and Technology

Understand the role of information and technology in service creation, delivery and support.

Service Creation

Discover how to design and develop services that meet customer needs and fulfill business objectives.

Service Delivery and Support

Learn the processes and activities to deliver high-quality IT services. Identify the support functions and practices to maintain and manage IT services effectively.

Managing Suppliers

Understand the commercial and sourcing considerations.
