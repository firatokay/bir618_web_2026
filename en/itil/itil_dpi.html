What will you learn?

This course helps to align product and service management with modern business requirements; drive successful organizational transformation; and embed continual improvement into an organization's behaviour at every level.

Key concepts

Gain a deeper understanding of the key concepts for direction, planning and improvement for IT and digital services.

Strategy and direction

Explore the creation and management of a service provider's strategy and providing guidance on directing activities.

Assessment and planning

Discover the activities to understand the current state and plan an improvement or other initiative.

Measurement and reporting

Define and use key performance indicators (KPIs) to assess and improve the performance and effectiveness of IT services and processes.

Continual improvement

Learn the concepts and practices of improvement to help services, processes and practices evolve.

Organizational change management

Focus on the importance of organizational change management to facilitate the successful delivery of services.

Developing a service value system

Explore the skills to develop a service value system with the usage of four dimensions.
