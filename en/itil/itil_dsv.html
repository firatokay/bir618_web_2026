What will you learn?

This course provides guidance on establishing, maintaining, and developing effective service relationships at appropriate levels. It leads organizations on a service journey in their service provider and consumer roles, supporting effective interaction and communication.

The Customer Journey

Gain a deeper understanding of the customer journey. Identify touchpoints and service interactions.

Explore and Engage

Identify, assess and prioritise the needs and expectations of various stakeholders, including customers, employees, partners and regulators. Foster positive stakeholder relationships through effective communication and collaboration.

Offer and Agree

Discover how to manage demand and design service offerings. Learn how to align expectations and establish a shared view of the target service scope and quality between the service provider and the service consumer.

Onboard and Co-create

Explore onboarding activities to enable service provision and consumption. Gain skills to ensure continual value co-creation based on agreed service offerings.

Realize

Develop the skills to track, assess and report service value realization.
