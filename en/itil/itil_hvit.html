What will you learn?

This course addresses the specifics of digital transformation and helps organizations to evolve towards a convergence of business and technology, or to establish a new digital organization.

Key concepts

Gain a deeper understanding of the key concepts of IT and digital service delivery in a high-speed world.

High-velocity IT culture

Explore the kind of culture that supports and enables high-velocity IT including key behaviour patterns.

Valuable investments

Identify and justify digital investments that would contribute to business strategy.

Fast development

Discover how to realize new and improved digital products and services frequently, quickly and reliably.

Resilient operations

Understand techniques to ensure digital products and services are available for use whenever needed.

Co-created value

Learn how to co-create value from digital products and services through close collaboration of the service provider and the consumer.

Assured conformance

Develop the skills to ensure service provision and consumption comply with corporate and regulatory directives with respect to governance, risk and compliance.
